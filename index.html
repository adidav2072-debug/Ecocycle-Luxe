Organisation Overview

EcoCycle Luxe is a creative studio started in 2022 because we hate seeing old technology end up in a bin. Instead of throwing away old circuit boards, wires, and metal parts, we reinvent them into modern décor and gallery-style sculptures.

Our mission is to prove that "waste" can be beautiful and valuable. We want to reach art collectors and stylish office owners in South Africa who want unique pieces that help save the planet.
