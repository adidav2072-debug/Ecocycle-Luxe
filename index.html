Organisation Overview

EcoCycle Luxe is a creative studio started in 2022. I started this because I hate seeing old technology end up in a bin and instead of throwing away old circuit boards, wires, and metal parts, we reinvent them into modern décor and gallery-style sculptures.

Our mission is to prove that "waste" can be beautiful and valuable. We want to reach art collectors and stylish office owners who want unique pieces that also help save the planet.
